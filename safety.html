Safety Tips

  • Always accompany young children during trick-or-treating.
  • Use reflective tape or glow sticks to enhance visibility.
  • Choose costumes that provide good visibility and mobility.
  • Be aware of how your vision is affected by props and masks.
  • Stick to well-lit areas and use sidewalks when walking.
  • Look both ways before crossing streets and use designated crosswalks.
  • Be cautious of traffic and remind children to stay close to you.
  • Inspect all treats before consuming, discard any unwrapped or home-brewed treats.
  • Keep costumes away from open flames or candles.
  • Teach children to never enter a stranger's home or vehicle.
  • Carry a flashlight to improve visibility and make yourself more visible to others.

Remember, prioritizing safety ensures a fun and enjoyable Halloween for everyone involved!
